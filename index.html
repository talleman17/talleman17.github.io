The heart of Hallownest, the great city of the fallen, let it envelope you in a sweet embrace.

Hollow Knight is a 2D side-scrolling Metroidvania game where players control a silent insectoid protagonist exploring the vast and interconnected world of Hallownest. The game features a mix of friendly and hostile insectoid characters, numerous bosses, and a rich lore system. Players can unlock abilities, collect pieces of lore, and enjoy the game's music, art style, and atmosphere. The game has been praised for its level of difficulty and has sold over 15 million copies.
